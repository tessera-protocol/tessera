The Roman tessera hospitalis was a clay tile broken between two strangers. Each carried a half; reuniting the pieces proved identity and established trust across distance.

Three converging crises

The internet cannot verify humans, authorise agents, or prove who made what.

01

Fake identity costs nothing

A single actor can create thousands of identities at zero cost. CAPTCHAs and phone verification test capability, not identity. Sybil attacks remain trivially cheap.

02

Agents can't prove who sent them

AI agents browse, transact, and communicate autonomously but have no standard way to prove a human authorised them, what they're permitted to do, or when that permission expires.

03

AI content is flooding everything

Text, images, music, and video generated by AI are indistinguishable from human work. There is no tool-agnostic way to prove human authorship without revealing identity.

Four layers

Each layer is independently useful and composable into the full protocol.

01

Anchor

Root of trust via bank KYC, card verification, phone, or verification network. Multiple anchors stack to increase credential strength. No data stored after issuance.

02

Credential

A W3C Verifiable Credential proving "verified unique human" with zero-knowledge proofs via Semaphore. No name, email, or PII included. Stored on your device.

03

Delegation

Humans create scoped, time-limited, revocable wallets for their AI agents. Ed25519-signed delegations with scope containment. Revoking a parent invalidates all children.

04

Verification

Open-source SDK for platforms. Verify a credential in under 200ms: ZK proof, issuer signature, trusted group root, nullifier check, revocation status.

// platform integration 
import { createVerifier } from 'tessera-sdk';

const verifier = createVerifier({
  platformId: 'my-platform',
  trustedIssuerPublicKeys: [issuerPubKey],
  trustedGroupRoots: [currentRoot],
});

const result = await verifier.verify(proof);
// { valid: true, type: 'human', tier: 1, scope: null }

Two verticals

Agent identity and human provenance. Both stem from the same credential.

Agent identity

The first wallet system for AI agents. Create a passport for your agent, set what it can do, export a credential token. Integrate with MCP servers, OpenClaw, Claude Code, or any compatible framework.

Human provenance

Sign your creative work with a zero-knowledge proof of human authorship. Prove "a real person made this" without revealing who. Tool-agnostic, medium-agnostic, privacy-preserving.
